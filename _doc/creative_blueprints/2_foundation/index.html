The Problem

You're on call around the clock — for cargo questions.

Buyers and partners message your WhatsApp group at all hours: "Is cargo #12 ready?", "What are the specs for this lot?", "When does the shipment arrive?" Every answer requires you at the keyboard — manually looking up data and typing replies. No system handles it. No one sleeps.

The Solution

An AI agent that lives in your WhatsApp group and answers for you.

Update your cargo data once in a dashboard. The agent reads every message, checks the live records, and replies instantly — 24/7, no human in the loop. Routine queries are handled automatically. Complex ones are escalated to you. You close the laptop.
