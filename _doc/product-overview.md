# Product Overview

## What It Is
An AI-powered WhatsApp cargo agent for palm oil refinery businesses. The agent sits inside the operator's WhatsApp buyer/partner group 24/7 — automatically reading incoming cargo questions and responding with accurate data pulled from a central cargo dashboard. No human on call required for routine queries.

## The Problem
Palm oil refinery operators manage buyer and partner relationships through WhatsApp group chats. Cargo queries arrive around the clock — "Is cargo #12 ready?", "What are the specs for this lot?", "When does the shipment arrive?" — and every one requires a human to look up the data and type a reply manually. The operator is effectively on call 24 hours a day just to keep the group answered, unable to step away without messages piling up and buyers waiting.

## The Solution
An AI agent embedded in the WhatsApp group that monitors messages and auto-answers cargo queries. The operator updates cargo status, specs, ETAs, and shipment details once in a management dashboard. The agent handles all routine questions instantly from that live data — around the clock, without the operator at the keyboard. Complex or unusual queries are flagged for human follow-up.

## Core Capabilities
- **WhatsApp Group Integration**: AI agent monitors the buyer/partner WhatsApp group and responds to incoming cargo queries automatically, 24/7
- **Cargo Management Dashboard**: Central interface for the operator to enter and update cargo records — status, specifications, ETAs, and shipment details
- **Auto-Response Engine**: Reads buyer and partner questions in natural language and delivers instant, accurate answers drawn from live cargo data
- **Natural Language Understanding**: Parses varied question phrasing across languages and matches queries to the correct cargo record
- **Exception Escalation**: Questions outside the agent's confidence are flagged for the operator to handle personally
- **Full Audit Log**: Every question asked and answer given is recorded — complete accountability and review trail

## Target Users
**Primary user**: Palm oil refinery business owner/operator — the person who updates cargo data, reviews escalated queries, and owns the dashboard.
**End recipients**: Buyers, logistics partners, and trading contacts in the WhatsApp group — they interact via WhatsApp and receive automated responses.

## Operational Context
A typical refinery WhatsApp buyer group receives an estimated 15–30 cargo queries per day across buyers and partners. Each manual response takes approximately 5–15 minutes of operator time (lookup + reply). Routine queries (cargo readiness, specs, ETA) represent approximately 85–90% of total message volume; the remainder require human judgment.

## Positioning
An operational automation tool for palm oil refinery businesses running buyer and partner communications through WhatsApp. Eliminates 24/7 manual cargo query answering. Built for the refinery operator who needs to sleep without buyers waiting on answers.

## Tone & Brand
Direct, professional, results-focused. Speaks to the refinery operator in plain business terms — not a tech product pitch, a business operations tool. Calm confidence.
